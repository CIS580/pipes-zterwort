Play Pipes

Instructions

  • Pipes available to you will be shown on the left, in order of use from buttom to top, highligting the pipe which is next in line to use.
  • Ooze will immediately start flowing through the pipes, so place your pipes fast!
  • The object of the game is to connect the start and ending nodes.
  • The faster you connect the pipes, the more points you will receive.
  • If a pipe is finished being filled with ooze, and there isn't another pipe to fill, you lose.
  • Connect the nodes, and fill the pipes with ooze to win!
  • GOOD LUCK!
